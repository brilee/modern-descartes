Hi there!

I'm Brian Lee. I'm currently a joint graduate student under Professors Mahadevan and Szostak at Harvard, having graduated from MIT in 2011 with a degree in Chemistry. I study lipid membrane biomechanics, and I also dabble in mathematical modeling of various sorts.

My interests include: math, physics, programming, Go, chemistry, piano, and teaching.

I write various essays on my personal thoughts. I also explore interesting technical problems from a pedagogical perspective.

I'm one of the founding members of the American Collegiate Go Association, which is an association dedicated to spreading Go via college Go clubs.

I represented the U.S. at the International Chemistry Olympiad in 2007, and I served as peer mentor for the US National Chemistry Olympiad Study Camp in 2010-2012.
